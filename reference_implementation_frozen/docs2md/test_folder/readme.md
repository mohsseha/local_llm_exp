## About Generate:Biomedicines

Generate:Biomedicines is a biotechnology company pioneering a field known as "Generative Biology." The company's mission is to revolutionize drug development by using artificial intelligence and machine learning to program novel protein-based therapeutics. Their proprietary platform, known as Chroma, is capable of learning the complex principles of protein structure and function. This allows the company to generate a wide array of potential medicines, including antibodies, enzymes, and peptides, that are custom-designed to target the root cause of various diseases.

Founded in 2018 by Flagship Pioneering, Generate:Biomedicines has established itself as a significant player in the biotech industry. The company operates from its headquarters in Somerville, Massachusetts, and has expanded its physical footprint with state-of-the-art laboratory and cryo-EM facilities in Andover. It has secured substantial funding and formed strategic collaborations with major pharmaceutical companies and research institutions, such as Amgen and The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center. These partnerships are aimed at accelerating the development of its pipeline, which spans across immunology, oncology, and infectious diseases.
